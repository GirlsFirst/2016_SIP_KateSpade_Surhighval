This website is to help high school students survive high school!


Rumors and Truths Told to Me During High School:

  • There is no pool on the roof! Do not believe this story no matter how convincing people sound telling it to you!


  • School lunch is not poison it is just gross.


  • Seniors will not beat you up they are just tall.


  • For freshman, if you feel like people are annoyed by you they are.

    There is just something about freshman that annoy people. Trust me you will feel the same way when you are not one anymore.


  • Do not do projects of any kind the night before. Sadly like the rest of us you will probably

    end up doing this anyway and will have to learn the hard way.


  • If you get to pick your seats on the first day of class and you want to avoid getting called on then

    try to sit in the middle and the opposite side that the teachers desk is on.

    This way you'll be out of the teachers sight and you'll blend into the crowd.


  • Make sure you have all of your supplies on the first day of school

    because nobody likes that one person who is always asking for a pencil or paper.


  • Use a planner religiously because you will never forget to do an assignment again.


  • Time management will be your best friend in high school! If you can manage

    your time well then you're set. We cannot stress this enough.


  • Be nice to teachers. You do not want to get on their bad side. They're deciding your grade after all.


  • Don't sit next to people that will distract you (even if they're your friends) because your grade will suffer


  • If you miss a day of school, make sure you contact your teachers or friends to get your missing assignments and homework.


  • Don't be afraid to ask questions in class if you don't understand something.

    Having no idea what you're doing is worst than asking for help.


  • Get the phone numbers of people in your class so you can ask them homework questions.

    Also, if you're ever late to class they can tell the teacher for you.


  • Try to add some fun classes to your schedule so you're not totally stressed out with all of your AP classes
